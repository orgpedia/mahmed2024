3) The proposed purchase rates are approximate when given administrative approval. Hence, the prices should be fixed at the end of the procurement process. 4) The specifications of the equipment (80911008 specification) BIH, are comprehensive and the equipment supply WHI should be of a rigid type with at least three tenders from three manufacturers / suppliers. Also, the procurement process should be done only after verifying that the technical specification is not designed to benefit any particular company.
5) The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Dean concerned shall ensure that the proposed equipment is not included in any proposal elsewhere / is not allowed to be purchased and that the equipment is required by the institution concerned.
6) While deciding on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of grant-in-aid sanctioned under the head of accounting provided for administrative approval.
