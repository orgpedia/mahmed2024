Conditions: - 1) This administrative approval is being granted to the institution concerned in the financial year 2025-26 out of the funds sanctioned and available under the State Schemes.
The Government has issued a "Manual of Procedure for Office Procurement to be carried out by the Government Department," issued by the Department of Industries, Power and Labour, Government of India in this regard.
The procedures mentioned in the government decisions should be followed.
The proposed purchase rates are approximate when given administrative approval. Hence, the prices should be fixed at the end of the procurement process. The Commissioner (Medical Education) as well as the Principal concerned shall ensure that the rate is less than the market price and not more than the rate at which the concerned supplier has supplied these items elsewhere.
The specifications of the equipment (8091110081 specification) ATH, are comprehensive and accordingly the equipment supplier AHIR should be of such type that at least three tenders can be received from three manufacturers / suppliers. Also, these technical specifications will benefit any particular company, the procurement process should be done only after it is confirmed that there is no stan-ready HSS.
The installer will be responsible for ensuring that the machine meets the approved specifications when installed.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Principal concerned shall ensure that the proposed equipment is not included in any other proposal / purchase and that the equipment is required by the institution concerned.
While deciding on the purchase of machinery in this regard, it should be ensured that the expenditure does not exceed the provision of grant-in-aid sanctioned under the head of accounts provided for administrative approval.
It will be necessary to show the prices of the main equipment and their accessories as different.
It should be ensured that no new designations are required for this equipment.
