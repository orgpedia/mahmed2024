The proposed purchase rates are approximate when given administrative approval. Hence, the prices should be fixed at the end of the procurement process. The Commissioner (Medical Education) as well as the Administrator concerned shall ensure that the rate is below the market price and does not exceed the rate at which the supplier concerned has supplied these items elsewhere.
The specifications of the equipment (18911108) should be comprehensive, comprehensive and of such a nature that three tenders can be received from at least three manufacturers / suppliers who can supply the equipment accordingly. Also, the procurement process should be done only after verifying that the technical specifications are not designed to benefit any particular company.
While installing the equipment, it shall be the responsibility of the concerned administrator to ensure that all the equipment is as per the specifications.
