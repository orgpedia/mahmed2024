The procedures mentioned in the government decisions should be followed.
The proposed purchase rates are estimated at the time of administrative approval. Hence, the prices should be fixed at the end of the procurement process. The Commissioner (Medical Education) as well as the Administrator concerned shall ensure that the rate is below the market price and does not exceed the rate at which the supplier concerned has supplied these items elsewhere.
The technical specification of the equipment should be comprehensive, comprehensive and in accordance with which the equipment supplier WHI should be able to receive three bids from at least three manufacturers / suppliers. Also, the procurement process should be done only after verifying that the technical specification is not designed to benefit any particular company.
It shall be the responsibility of the Dean to ensure that the project is being set up as per the specifications.
The Commissioner (Medical Education) and the Principal concerned shall ensure that the proposed equipment is not included in any proposal elsewhere / is not allowed to be purchased and that the equipment is required by the institution concerned.
