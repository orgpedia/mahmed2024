The Proposal: -
Reference No. The Government Decision dated 1st April, 2020 has approved the establishment of a Government Medical College with a capacity of 100 students and an attached 430-bed hospital at Washim. Government Medical College, Washim is an important government medical institution established to cater to the medical education and healthcare needs of the citizens of Washim district and surrounding rural and tribal areas. The college provides secondary and tertiary healthcare to the general public along with medical education. The government has received representations from public representatives regarding the registration of this government medical college. Sant Sewalal Maharaj is known as the revered god of the Banjara community and a great social reformer. He promoted social equality, ethical conduct, de-addiction, hard work, self-reliance, and human values. His work has promoted social awareness, self-respect, and righteousness in society, and the influence of his thoughts is still widely seen today. The government was considering the issue of enrolment of this government medical college in Washim district which catered to the medical education needs.
The Government's Decisions:
The Government is approving the registration of Government Medical College, Washim, under the Directorate of Medical Research, as "Sant Sewalal Maharaj Government Medical College, Wathim."
